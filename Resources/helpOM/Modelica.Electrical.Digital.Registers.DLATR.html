Truth Table for high active reset:

DataIn Enable Reset DataOut Map
* * U U 1
* * 1 0 2
* 0 0 NC 3
* 1 0 DataIn 3
* X 0 X or U or NC 3
* U ~1 U 4
* ~U X X or U or 0 or NC 4

Truth Table for low active reset:

DataIn Enable Reset DataOut Map
* * U U 1
* * 0 0 2
* 0 1 NC 3
* 1 1 DataIn 3
* X 1 X or U or NC 3
* U ~0 U 4
* ~U X X or U or 0 or NC 4 

  *  = do not care
  ~  = not equal
  U  = L.'U'
  0  = L.'0' or L.'L'
  1  = L.'1' or L.'H'
  X  = L.'X' or L.'W' or L.'Z' or L.'-'
  NC = no change
