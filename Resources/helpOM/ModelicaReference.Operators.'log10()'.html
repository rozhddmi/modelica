Information

Base 10 logarithm

Syntax

log10(u)

Description

Returns the base 10 logarithm of u, with u > 0. Argument u needs to be an Integer or Real expression.

The base 10 logarithm can also be accessed as Modelica.Math.log10.

Examples

log10(1)
 = 0
